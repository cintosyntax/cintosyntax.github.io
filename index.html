Who Am I?

Hey there!

My name is Edwin Mak (aka Cintosyntax) and I am a full-stack web developer currently living in New York City. My adventure in software engineering began in entrepreneurship that eventually lead me to become a founding engineer of a venture backed logistics company. I spend my days building my craft as a Software Engineer by tackling projects that range from infrastructure design to user interface development. My engineering approach is to favor pragmatism. I operate with a "get stuff done" attitude balanced with an appreciation on the importance of well designed code.


When I am not wearing my engineering hat, I spend my leisure time learning new technologies, scouring hacker news, practicing capoeira, or traveling.
